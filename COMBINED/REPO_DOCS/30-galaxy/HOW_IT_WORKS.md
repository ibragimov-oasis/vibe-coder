## What it does (plain language for vibe-coders)

A collection of over 3,000 unique UI elements from Uiverse.io — buttons, cards, loaders, toggles, checkboxes, inputs, switches, and more. All MIT licensed. Every element is sourced from the Uiverse.io platform where designers submit creations that are automatically curated and uploaded here. Browse at uiverse.io, copy HTML+CSS, and use directly.
